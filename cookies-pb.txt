Peanut Butter Cookies

For 16 large cookies:

100 g     Butter
150 g     PB
150 g     Sugar
1         Egg
150 g     Flour
1/2 tsp   Soda
1/2 tsp   Vanilla

Cream butter, sugar, PB and egg together. Sift in flour and soda. Add vanilla.
Spoon half-tablespoon sized lumps onto ungreased baking sheet.
Bake for 10-12 mins at 160 degrees Celcius.
Cool first on tray before transferring to rack.
